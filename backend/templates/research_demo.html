Indoor Farming Study Case — Suggested Videos

The backend cannot search YouTube directly. Use these search queries to find suitable footage. Paste the URL into the field above and click Load.

Vertical Farm Tour

Camera walkthrough of a vertical indoor farm with LED grow lights, plant shelves, and hydroponic trays. Look for AeroFarms, Bowery Farming, or Plenty tours.

YouTube: "indoor vertical farm tour walkthrough"

Hydroponic Greenhouse

Camera moving through rows of hydroponic plant channels. Shows grow lights, nutrient pipes, sensors. Visible YOLO classes: person, potted plant, bottle, clock.

YouTube: "hydroponic greenhouse inside tour"

Japan Plant Factory

Japanese plant factory tours often show dense LED-lit shelves with leafy greens. Phase 1 may recognize as "greenhouse" or "laboratory" — interesting edge case.

YouTube: "Japan plant factory indoor farming"

AgriForce / Freight Farms

Container farms or modular indoor growing systems. Compact environment with monitoring screens, control panels. May trigger "server_room" or "laboratory" scene recognition.

YouTube: "freight farm container grow inside tour"
Expected hypothesis: Places365 GoogLeNet may not have a specific "indoor farm" category. Phase 1 will likely classify as greenhouse, vegetable_garden, botanical_garden, or laboratory. WorldV2 context classes will adapt accordingly — revealing how the framework handles environments not well-represented in the Places365 training set. This is a valuable negative/edge-case result.
